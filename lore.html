The World of Aethermoor

Aethermoor is a realm of ancient magic and broken promises. Once a land of thriving kingdoms and divine order, it has spent three centuries slowly unraveling since the event known as the Sundering.

The gods did not die — they simply stopped answering. No one knows why. What followed was panic, war, and the rise of the Voidclaw: a creeping darkness that devours both land and memory. Entire cities have been swallowed. Some people claim to have escaped from inside it. Most of them are not well.

What remains of Aethermoor is still worth fighting for. Three kingdoms still stand, and within each of them, heroes are trained, forged, and sent out to push back the dark.

The Three Hero Classes

🛡️

Warrior

Warriors are the backbone of every army in Aethermoor. Trained since childhood in the art of combat, they carry heavy armor and heavier responsibilities. When the line breaks, a warrior does not run — they become the new line.

Strengths: High defense, great endurance, reliable in any fight.
Weaknesses: Limited magic ability, slower movement.
Signature weapon: Sword and shield.

🔮

Mage

Mages study for decades to master the arcane arts — and even then, the magic is never fully tamed. They are fragile in body but devastating in battle. A single mage can level a building. Two mages once stopped a river.

Strengths: Extremely powerful spells, wide area of effect.
Weaknesses: Low health, vulnerable up close.
Signature weapon: Staff of the Void.

🏹

Hunter

Hunters grew up in the wilderness, learning to survive before they learned to read. They track, they trap, and they strike from shadows. Most enemies never know a hunter was there until it is far too late.

Strengths: High agility, stealth, long-range attacks.
Weaknesses: Low armor, weaker in direct confrontations.
Signature weapon: Enchanted longbow.

The Three Kingdoms

Ironheld

The fortress-kingdom of the north. Ironheld is built into a mountain range and has never fallen to siege. It is home to most of Aethermoor's warriors and is governed by a council of veteran generals.

Velmoor

A city of towers and libraries, Velmoor is where mages are trained and magical artifacts are studied. It sits at the center of the continent, surrounded by protective wards that have held for two hundred years.

The Ashwood

Not a kingdom in the traditional sense — the Ashwood is a vast forest with no capital and no throne. Hunters move through it freely, living in small groups and answering to no one but each other.

A Brief History

300 years ago

The Sundering

The gods fall silent overnight. No prayers are answered. No divine magic works. Chaos follows.

280 years ago

The First Voidclaw Sighting

A village in the eastern plains disappears without a trace. The darkness has a name.

200 years ago

The Three Kingdoms Form

The surviving city-states unite into Ironheld, Velmoor, and the Ashwood. The hero training programs begin.
