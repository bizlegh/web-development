Welcome to Aethermoor

Three centuries ago, the old gods fell silent. Since then, the realm of Aethermoor has been slowly consumed by a darkness known as the Voidclaw. Cities have crumbled, roads are dangerous, and ancient pacts have been broken.

But in the ruins of the old world, heroes rise. Warriors, mages, and hunters who stand between what remains and what threatens to take it all.

Aethermoor does not need perfection. It needs you.
